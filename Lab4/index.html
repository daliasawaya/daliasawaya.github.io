Our Services


Pelvic Health

60$ for 30 minutes

Our Pelvic Health Physiotherapists have advanced training and are specialists in resolving pelvic floor dysfunctions, as well as providing pre- and post-natal care.

Dry Needling

120$ for 45 minutes

Our Physiotherapists are trained to administer trigger point dry needling, as well as traditional Chinese acupuncture, to treat a variety of conditions allowing a faster return to pain-free function.

Complete Concussion Management

100$ for 30 minutes

Our Physiotherapists are certified providers of Complete Concussion Management, allowing them to be at the forefront of concussion diagnosis, management, and rehabilitation.

Active Release Technique (ART)

200$ for 1 hour

Our Physiotherapists utilize this soft tissue system/movement based massage technique to treat problems with muscles, tendons, ligaments, fascia and nerves.

Shockwave Therapy

100$ for 30 minutes

Our Physiotherapists are trained in the use of Shockwave Therapy to treat conditions. Radial shockwaves help to support and reset the healing pattern in the affected tissue.

Yoga and Pilates

80$ for 1 hour

Our Physiotherapists are also certified yoga instructors, and combine their two areas of specialty into PhysioYoga classes. These classes are therapeutic movement classes and are both educational and fun!
